I have been studing photography for seven years. I am confident in the use of all formats of camera, studio equipment and any other photographic paraphernalia.

I have extensive experience of Adobe Photoshop, having taught myself to use it five years ago and have been using it in my photographic work ever since.

I have a good working knowledge of Quark Express, having used the program to create magazine articles, posters and flyers.

I became interested in web design some time ago. With no available guidance, I began pulling apart the code of interesting web sites to understand how HTML and Javascript function. As with Photoshop and Quark, my understanding of web design remains self taught.

For the last three years my work has been concerned with photographing the underground dance scene of the North-East. For some time I have also been photographing various aspects of DiY Culture, GM crop demonstrations, anti-capitalist demonstrations, squatters and travellers, etc. in order to compile a web based 'DiY Culture Manual', a guide for the uninitiated and a self-affirmation for those involved. This is an ongoing project.

My partner and I are expecting a baby at the end of October, and have set up home in Barry. I am looking for work in the South Wales area.

Aside from those interests already mentioned, I have a fascination for cacti, collecting new plants whenever possible. I also enjoy hitching, finding it one of the most enjoyable ways of getting from A to B at a leisurely pace.
